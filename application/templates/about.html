About

Invoice Manager is an application that helps you organize your profits and expenses. It will make financial management in your company as easy as pie. You can create a user account, log in and add your invoices to the app.

4th semester university project, for Script Languages course. Created with Python and Flask




Disclaimer: The invoices uploaded to the app are fake and are not valid in law. They have been generated with Visual Basic script and are used only for the educational purposes. All the data used in the invoices are fictional and do not represent any real person or company. {% endblock content %}
